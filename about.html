About Asiyah's Spacecraft

Crafting Timeless Celebrations

At Asiyah’s Spacecraft, we believe every event is a story waiting to be beautifully told. Our team specializes in creating luxurious, seamless experiences that reflect your unique vision. From intimate weddings to grand soirées, we curate each detail with elegance, precision, and passion.

With years of experience and a portfolio of stunning events, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and unforgettable memories. Your dream day is our mission — and we’re here to make it magical.

We specialize in uniquely personalized designs & precise execution to ensure your event is a memory that gets talked about for years. (Not just for you alone—but all your guests!) At Asiyah’s Spacecraft,, we bring together experiences in the design, production, and orchestration of corporate events, private parties, and wedding celebrations in traditional and unexpected venues throughout Abuja, Lagos, and beyond. As a premier event planner, our team specializes in leaving no detail untouched, turning an event date into a one-of-a-kind event. From vineyards to private estates, we transform even the most unexpected spaces and redefine the planning and design process to create unforgettable, immersive experiences. Simply put, every event we plan is custom—the only replication is the proven process we’ve developed over time to ensure flawless events. Our designs feature crisp, clean lines and an inviting residential style that prioritizes the flow of the event for an impeccable guest experience. Our style isn’t the only thing that commands the respect of our clients & vendor partners. If there's one thing we hear about most, it’s our ability to anticipate challenges and navigate the complexities of event coordination with grace under pressure.
